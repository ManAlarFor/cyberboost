Characteristics

In most cyberpunk worlds, its control is shared between some corporation, whose power increased after gaining loads of money thanks to the monopolies. This corps, sometimes, even have armies for themselves, controling even more the cities and population. Some examples are, for example, Arasaka, from the game Cyberpunk 2077, whose tower and be seen in the picture next to this text. A lot of people think that this could be our future.

Another thing that is as recurrent as the corporations in this genre, is the relationship between life and technology. In this worlds the quality of life is often incredibly low, having to deal with gangs and mafias everyday in a ultracapitalist society. The main contrast, is that the access to technology is pretty easy, having almost everyone implants that make their life "easier".
